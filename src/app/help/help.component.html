Get started with the FitBitTeam Website


You can navigate through the site using the navigation bar above.
To use this website, you can signup here!

You then want to link your Fitbit account by going to the settings bar above and click the "Login to Fitbit" button.
Now you can view your weight trends for the past 30 days, add medications, and view reccomendations on your timeline.

Check the Account and Connect tabs for more information.

Manage your Accounts


Edit your details
Once you created your account using the sign up page, you can manage details in the settings page.
You can "Login to FitBit" or "Revoke Access", and edit your first and last name.

To change your password, please click "Reset Password" or click here.

FitBit Accounts
Once you are logged into FitBit, your access token and id will be stored and displayed in the settings page.
If the "Revoke Access" button does not work, navigate to FitBit: Manage Applications or login to Fitbit,
navigate to your settings, applications, and revoke access for "Capstone490 by UoSC Weight Management App".

Check the Get Started and Connect tabs for more information.

Connect to your Doctor


To connect to your doctor make sure to follow the Get Started tab and connect your FitBit account to the website.

Navigate to the settings page and write down your referral code listed at the bottom.
Give your Doctor your referral code via personal email or text and they will connect with you.
