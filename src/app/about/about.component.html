About our Mission

Our mission is to help those struggling with congestive heart failure by enabling them to manage their weight, medications, and doctor's reccomendations through the seemless integration of the Fitbit devices and Amazon Alexa.

Meet the Developers

This project was for our senior capstone design class. To learn more about the program click here. Chris, Mikhail, Tyler, Chase, and Matt all worked on this webapp for Professor Corbett at the University of South Carolina. We are using the Fitbit API and Amazon Alexa to pull data and later with voice integration. If you have any questions about the website, please use the contact page. Our github repo is available here.

Privacy Policy

At FitBitTeam, accessible from fitbittesterv2.herokuapp.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by FitBitTeam and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Log Files

FitBitTeam follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Data Collection

FitBitTeam captures data through the FitBit sdk, and signup page. All usernames, emails, and passwords are safely stored using Google's Firebase. Once a user authorizes use of their fitbit weight data, we store this data on a per-user basis to populate timelines. To delete this data, simply revoke access to your Fitbit account.
